Salsa Verde

Description

Salsa verde is a tangy and flavorful green sauce that originates from Mexico. It's made primarily from tomatillos, which are small green tomatoes with a tart flavor, along with other ingredients like jalapeños, onions, cilantro, and lime juice.

Ingredients

  • Tomatillos These small, green, tomato-like fruits are the primary ingredient in salsa verde, providing a tangy flavor and vibrant green color.
  • Jalapeños These green chili peppers add heat and depth of flavor to the salsa verde. They can be adjusted to taste depending on desired spice level.
  • Cilantro Fresh cilantro leaves contribute a bright and herbaceous flavor to salsa verde, enhancing its freshness and complexity.

Steps

  1. Remove the husks from the tomatillos and rinse them under warm water to remove the sticky residue.
  2. Cut the tomatillos in half and place them cut side down on a baking sheet.
  3. Broil the tomatillos in the oven until they are soft and slightly charred, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, roast the jalapeños and serrano peppers under the broiler or on a grill until they are charred on all sides.
  5. Remove the stems from the peppers and place them in a blender or food processor.
  6. Add the roasted tomatillos, garlic, onion, cilantro, lime juice, and salt to the blender.
  7. Blend the ingredients until smooth, adding a little water if necessary to reach your desired consistency.
  8. Taste the salsa and adjust the seasoning as needed, adding more salt or lime juice if desired.
  9. Transfer the salsa to a bowl and let it cool to room temperature before serving.
  10. Enjoy your homemade salsa verde with tortilla chips or as a topping for your favorite Mexican dishes!
